SEASCAPE is building validated, non-invasive biodiversity intelligence for reefs and blue-forest ecosystems.

The problem

Restoration is growing faster than proof.

Unmet need

Coastal restoration still depends on short diver surveys, manual image review, and acoustic proxies that are promising but not yet consistently validated against biological reality.

Why it matters

Managers, regulators, NGOs, and blue-economy funders need credible, continuous biodiversity metrics to evaluate whether reefs, mangroves, kelp forests, and seagrass systems are actually recovering.

Scientific foundation

Sound changes behavior — but sound alone is not enough.

Target monitoring coverage for SEASCAPE’s synchronized visual-acoustic system.

Our insight is that ecoacoustics becomes much more valuable when paired with biological ground truth: who is present, in what abundance, at what size, and with what behavior when a soundscape changes.

The product

SEASCAPE converts reef observation into decision-ready data.

• 3 stereo-vision pairs capture 3D abundance, size, and movement.

• Directional hydrophones collect synchronized reef soundscapes.

• SLIP coatings and extended battery design support long deployments.

• AI pipelines automate fish detection, biomass estimates, and ecoacoustic validation.
